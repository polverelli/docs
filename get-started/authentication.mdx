Getting started with Lumx Protocol is quick and easy! Simply create your free account on our Dashboard and [grab your API key](https://dashboard.lumx.io/sandbox/settings/api-keys/) ). Within minutes, you'll be ready to harness the full power of our sandbox environment and start building amazing applications.

Ready to take your project to the next level? When you're prepared to move to production, reach out to our friendly team on [our community](https://join.slack.com/t/lumxprotocol/shared_invite/zt-2r5c6zxk3-5QGGZ3mUtLel5te1dI) - we're here to ensure your smooth transition to a production environment!
